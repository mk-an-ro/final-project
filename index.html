About Me

  • My passion has always been involved in creative arts. My dream has been to create beautiful things and makeup artistry afforded me that opportunity. Makeup teaches you to be flexible and to have the ability to pivot or change when needed. After years in the industry and the ever changing landscapes I have decided to take my creative passions and use them in the digital world.

    I am ever growing and changing and hope to continue to try to feed my insatiable hunger for knowledge and growth. Over the past few years, I have been learning HTML,CSS and Javascript to start my journey in to the tech world.

    I was given the opportunity to further my knowledge through the WebDesign and Software development course through UforChange. It has been a rewarding journey and I am grateful for what I have learned and for what is in store for the future!
